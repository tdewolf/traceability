<ul><li><strong>Flow / Context</strong>: Layout style for diagrams with a flow and a context.&nbsp;Nodes and edges in the flow are displayed mostly from left to right. The nodes and edges in a context are displayed above the flow.<br /><br /></li><li><strong>Hierarchy left -&gt; right</strong><span>: Nodes and edges are displayed in a flow from left to right. This is the default setting.</span><br /><span><br /></span></li><li><strong>Hierarchy top -&gt; down</strong>: Nodes and edges are displayed in a flow from top to bottom.<br /><br /></li><li><strong>Hierarchy bottom -&gt; up</strong>: Nodes and edges are displayed in a flow from bottom to top.<br /><br /></li><li><strong>Circular</strong>:&nbsp;<span style="color: rgb(64,64,64);">Nodes and edges are&nbsp;</span>&nbsp;<span style="color: rgb(64,64,64);">arranged in a radial tree.</span><br /><span style="color: rgb(64,64,64);"><br /></span></li><li><strong>Radial</strong>:&nbsp;<span style="color: rgb(64,64,64);">Nodes and edges are displayed with no overlaps, few edge crossings and few bends.</span><br /><span style="color: rgb(64,64,64);"><br /></span></li><li><strong>Smart Organic</strong>:&nbsp;<span style="color: rgb(64,64,64);">Nodes and edges are distributed in a well-balanced manner, there are few edge crossings.</span><span style="color: rgb(64,64,64);"><br /></span></li></ul>
